MAKE DIGITAL SOLUTIONS WORK FOR YOUR BUSINESS

Every business has digital potential, and we are here to help you leverage that potential. The right type of innovation gives you a competitive advantage and sets your business apart. Our strategic, experimental approach and fast execution are a solid recipe for building an innovation culture and making new digital offerings that let you keep your core business in focus.

Multidisciplinary team

Our team has all the necessary skills to drive innovation – from research, data analysis and strategy to design, video production. Technology is at the core of our DNA, and our internal development team enables us to move swiftly and be at the forefront of technology.

Innovation mindset

Driving innovation is not the same as engineering-style problem-solving. We have an open and creative approach, but also a clear strategic focus that drives the innovation process and gives the results business relevance and value.

Agile execution

Traditional planning frameworks do not offer the speed and flexibility needed to support innovation. We have developed an organization and structure that allow us to move quickly and seize new opportunities with our partners, with great agility and at an unparalleled speed.

Experiment Frequently

Gaining insights and generating early excitement about a new initiative is paramount, and a successful project requires that frequent experiments be conducted before the build phase gets underway – internally and externally.

FROM START-UPS TO GLOBAL BRANDS

Signifly and Maersk partnered to re-invent container sales with the first-ever online container sales platform. The goal is to make used containers easily available to businesses around the world, effectively and at an unprecedented speed. Work continues to improve the platform and its capabilities, and the solution is already generating substantial revenue and gaining recognition from clients, staff and industry peers.
Established in 1919 LLarsen Watches is one of Denmark’s oldest watchmakers. We brought them into the 21st century, but stayed true to their heritage, by visualising and telling about the Danish mindset and lifestyle. With a new visual identity, engaging online shopping platform and adverts for TV, social media and more, we used a new vision and contemporary approach to take a century-old fairy tale and turn it into a modern, desirable lifestyle that unifies the product and its history. The brand paved the way for entry into the Asian market, telling a compelling story with a strong base.
BotXO delivers a platform that empowers brands to have specialized chatbots providing instant support and personalised shopping through conversational interfaces. We helped them with a visual identity embracing their core business and designed a new website presenting Chatbot-technology to the world.

OUR VALUES

Honesty

Second agendas kill creativity. So openness, fairness and respect are qualities of the people we like to work with. We do our best to be transparent and honest and expect people around us to hold the same standards.

Energy

A positive attitude fuels creative processes, and good vibes tend to multiply when everyone brings their A-game. No one is perfect, but we do our best to keep the energy high and the vibe positive for ourselves and the teams we work with.

Fun

We love what we do and believe we do our best work when we have fun doing it. It’s not that we need to crack jokes or horse around – we just have to structure our efforts so what we’re doing feels more like play than work.
